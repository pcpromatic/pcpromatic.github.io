Step-by-Step Guide: Install PC Matic on This Computer Using an Existing Account and License

Below mentioned are the steps to get you started with pc matic:

Step 1: How to Log Into My PC Matic Account?

Before you can download or install anything, you need to access your PC Matic account. To do this, open your browser and go to pcmatic.com/account. This is the official login page where you can manage your devices, download software, and view your license information.

  • Enter your registered email address and password.
  • If you’ve forgotten your credentials, use the password reset option to regain access.

This step is essential, as you'll need access to your account to download the PC Matic software and retrieve your license key if you don’t have it on hand.

Step 2: Download PC Matic Already Purchased

Once you're logged into your account at pcmatic.com/account, navigate to the download section. Look for the option to download PC Matic already purchased. You don’t need to re-buy the software; your purchase allows multiple installations (depending on your license type).

Click the download link, and save the installer file to your computer. This file is usually small and should download quickly, depending on your internet speed.

Step 3: Install PC Matic on This Computer

Now that the installer has been downloaded, you're ready to install PC Matic on this computer. Locate the downloaded file (usually named something like PCmaticSetup.exe), then follow these steps:

  1. Double-click the installer file to begin the setup.
  2. When prompted, allow the installer to make changes to your device.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.

Make sure no other antivirus programs are running, as they can interfere with the installation.

Step 4: Install PC Matic with License Key

During or immediately after installation, the program may ask for your license key. This key confirms your purchase and activates your protection.

To install PC Matic with a license key:

  • Copy your license key from your account dashboard at pcmatic.com/account.
  • Paste the key into the required field when prompted during the installation.
  • Click “Activate” or “Submit” to register your product.

If the software doesn’t prompt you during installation, you can enter the license key from within the application after setup is complete.

Step 5: Install PC Matic with Account Credentials

Alternatively, if your license key is tied to your account, you can install PC Matic with your account login information instead:

  1. Open PC Matic after installation.
  2. Click on the login option.
  3. Enter the same email and password used for pcmatic.com/account.
  4. PC Matic will automatically detect your license and activate your installation.

This method is often easier if you don’t have your license key handy.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter any issues during the installation:

  • Double-check that you're using the correct account credentials.
  • Make sure your license is still active by visiting your dashboard.
  • Disable any conflicting security software temporarily during installation.
  • Restart your computer and try reinstalling if an error occurs.
